rocket_launch Version 1.0.0
Initial release

celebration Initial Release

  • Field-by-field comparison - Compare any order between Odoo and Shopify, field by field, right where you work.
  • Global verdict - A clear match or mismatch verdict for the whole order at a glance.
  • Net invoiced amount - The verdict is based on Odoo's net invoiced amount (invoices minus refunds), so partial invoices and refunds don't look like errors.
  • Discrepancy breakdown - Detailed differences across totals, taxes, quantities, line items and addresses.
  • Context for changes - Refunds, returns and order edits are shown in context, with detection of cancelled orders.
  • Zero-config autodetection - The Odoo instance and database are detected automatically from your active session — nothing to register.
  • Settings home - An icon popup and a panel gear let you activate a license, buy, manage your subscription and view read-only diagnostics.

workspace_premium Trial & Licensing

  • 14-day free trial - Every install includes a 14-day free trial with full functionality.
  • Starter, Growth and Agency plans - After the trial, choose Starter, Growth or Agency — company-wide plans scoped by Odoo instance and Shopify stores, licensed via Polar.
